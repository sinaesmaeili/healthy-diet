What is "Healthy Eating"?

  • Eating a variety and well balance of foods that give you proper nutrients and vitamins to maintain and boost your health
  • Being aware of food portions and calorie intake for your body. Calculate your TDEE and figure out your calories needed to maintain, gain or lose weight! tdeecalculator.net
  • Eat foods with little to no added sodium, sugars or saturated fat
  • Replace sugary high calorie drinks with water
  • Source: food-guide.canada.ca/en/healthy-eating-recommendations/

Alarming Obesity Facts

  • Number of Canadians that are overweight or obese has increased over the past 25 years
  • Two out of three adults in Canada are overweight or obese
  • Many factors have led to this increase such as society, work, and leisure which affect physical activity and eating patterns
  • Source: www.canada.ca/en/health-canada/services/healthy-living/your-health/lifestyles/obesity.html#mi

Health Risks of Obesity

  • High blood pressure
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Gallbladder disease
  • Coronary heart disease
  • Sleep apnea and other breathing problems
  • Source: www.canada.ca/en/health-canada/services/healthy-living/your-health/lifestyles/obesity.html#mi

High Sodium Intake in Canada and Its Consequences

  • Canadians eat 3400mg of sodium every day, which is more than double the amount needed
  • Sodium is essential in diets but must be consumed in moderation
  • Too much sodium leads to major risk for stroke, heart disease, stomach cancer, and kidney disease
  • Source: www.canada.ca/en/health-canada/services/food-nutrition/healthy-eating/sodium.html

So what are the benefits of eating properly and being a healthy weight?

  • Increase of energy
  • Improvement of mood
  • Reduced risk of disease
  • Boost in physique
  • Source: www.nwhu.on.ca/ourservices/HealthyLiving/Pages/Benefits-of-Healthy-Eating-and-Canada%27s-Food-Guide.aspx
